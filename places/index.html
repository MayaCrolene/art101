My first step was to make a website containing the history I felt was covered up/needed to be discussed more. I included brief informative text about the locations I chose and their narratives. I found additional websites I wanted to point readers to and linked them in my site. Next, I used a QR Code generator to link to my website. I printed these out with provocative messages next to them and traveled to the 5 locations. I put up the slips of paper and made a new email account. The last step is to pray these slips don't get torn down or rained on immediately so enough people can play a part in my project and hopefully learn something new or change their perspective.
